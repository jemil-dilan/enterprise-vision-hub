---
name: People Imagery Diversity
description: Casting/representation rule for any human imagery used across the site (team, hero, lifestyle, generated portraits)
type: design
---
People shown in website imagery must be predominantly Black, with occasional Chinese or White individuals appearing alongside. Apply this to:
- Team member photos and placeholders
- Hero and lifestyle photography
- Any AI-generated portraits or group shots
- Stock-style imagery in services/about sections

Exception: the founder portrait at src/assets/founder-portrait.png is a real uploaded photo and must not be replaced.
